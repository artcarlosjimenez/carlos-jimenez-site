Carlos
Jiménez
Fine Artist & Writer

Carlos Jiménez finds his greatest source of inspiration in love and life. His observation of the lives that surround him is the essential driving force that moves the artist, using pieces of memories and his heart — sometimes broken, sometimes filled with unforgettable memories.

His work is personal and very passionate, reflecting his sense of the search for a truth that may or may not exist. His philosophy is pragmatic, but with esoteric and passionate points of view about the meaning of life and the destiny of each person in the world.

“My problem is that I don’t believe in God, but I do believe in miracles.”
— Beigbeder
